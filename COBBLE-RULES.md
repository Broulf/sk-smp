# 📜 Cobblemon SMP Server Rules

Welcome to the **ScaredKitty Cobblemon SMP**!
This is a **community-driven survival + Pokémon experience**. Build, explore, and become the very best—together.

Please read and follow all rules to keep things fun and fair for everyone.

---

## 🌐 Server Information

* **Server IP:** `cobblemon.scaredkitty.net`
* **Modpack:** *BigChadGuysPlus w/ Cobblemon* (CurseForge)

---

## ⚖️ General Rules

### 1. No Unauthorized PvP

* **PvP is not allowed** unless:

  * Both players agree, **or**
  * It is part of an **organized event/tournament**
  * Cobblemon battles are encouraged—**player combat is not**

---

### 2. Respect Land & Builds

* Do **not build, mine, or modify** near another player’s base without permission
* If it looks like a base, **leave it alone**

---

### 3. Spawn Protection

* Do **not claim land within a 16 chunk radius of spawn**
* This area is reserved for:

  * Community builds
  * Gyms / Towns
  * Server events

---

### 4. No Griefing or Stealing

* Do not take or damage anything that isn’t yours
* “Pranks” that impact builds, Pokémon setups, or storage are **not allowed**

---

### 5. No Cheating or Exploits

* No:
  * X-ray
  * Dupes
  * Hacked clients
  * Game-breaking exploits
* If something feels unintended or overpowered, **ask staff first**

---

## 🧩 Cobblemon-Specific Rules

### 🎒 Pokémon & Battle Etiquette

* Do not:

  * Spam battle requests
  * Trap players in unwanted battles
  * Respect when someone declines a battle
  * No abusing mechanics to:
  * Crash battles
  * Soft-lock players
  * Duplicate Pokémon or items

---

### 🧬 Pokémon Spawns & Farms

* AFK farms for Pokémon must not:

  * Cause lag
  * Abuse spawn mechanics unfairly
* Staff may limit or remove problematic farms

---

### 🏟️ Gyms & Tournaments

* Player-made gyms are **encouraged!**
* Gym leaders should:

  * Be fair and consistent
  * Clearly state rules/rewards
* Server-hosted tournaments will have **custom rules announced per event**

---

## 🧩 Mod Usage Rules & Features

### 🏪 Player Shops

* Use shop systems provided in the modpack
* Be honest:

  * No misleading pricing
  * No scams
* Shops should be clearly labeled

---

### 🔒 Container Protection

* Use locking/protection mods included in the pack
* Do not:

  * Bypass protections
  * Pressure others for access

---

### 🧠 Quality of Life Mods

* Inventory sorting and other QoL features are allowed
* Do not install mods outside the official pack unless approved

---

### 🎙️ Voice Chat

* Proximity voice chat is enabled
* Keep it respectful:

  * No harassment
  * No mic spam

---

### 💬 Chat & Discord

* Chat is linked with Discord
* Follow all community rules in both places
* Be respectful and appropriate at all times

---

### ⚙️ Commands

* Common commands: `/spawn`, `/home`, `/tpa`, `/rtp`
* Do not use commands to:

  * Escape consequences unfairly
  * Gain unintended advantages

---

## 📦 Modpack Policy

* You must use the **BigChadGuysPlus w/ Cobblemon** modpack
* Only approved client-side QoL mods allowed
* If unsure → **ask staff**

---

## 🚨 Enforcement

Staff may:

* Remove builds
* Roll back damage
* Issue warnings, kicks, or bans

---

## ✅ Final Note

This server is about **community, creativity, and Pokémon adventure**.

If something feels:

* Unfair
* Exploitative
* Ruins the experience

…it’s probably not allowed.

**Have fun, train your team, and build something awesome! ⚡**
