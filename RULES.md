# 📜 Minecraft SMP Server Rules

Welcome to the **ScaredKitty SMP**!  
Please read and follow all rules to ensure a fun and fair experience for everyone.

---

## 🌐 Server Information
- **Server IP:** `scaredkitty.net`
- **Web Map:** https://map.scaredkitty.net
- **Modpack:** Official modpack available via CurseForge  
  Download here: https://curseforge.com

---

## ⚖️ General Rules

### 1. No PvP
- PvP is **not allowed** under any circumstances unless explicitly organized and agreed upon by all parties.
- This is a **community-focused survival server**, not a combat server.

---

### 2. Respect Land Claims
- Do **not build, mine, or modify** within another player’s claimed or established area without permission.
- If it looks like someone’s base, treat it as claimed.

---

### 3. Spawn Protection Radius
- Do **not claim land within a 16 chunk radius of spawn**.
- This area is reserved for community builds, infrastructure, and future expansion.

---

### 4. No Griefing or Stealing
- Do not destroy, alter, or take anything that does not belong to you.
- This includes “minor” trolling or pranks that affect builds or resources.

---

### 5. No Cheating or Exploits
- **No hacked clients, x-ray packs, dupes, or exploits.**
- The server runs **Anti-Xray**, and violations will be enforced.
- If you're unsure whether something is allowed, ask first.

---

## 🧩 Mod Usage Rules & Features

### 🏪 Universal Shops
- Players are encouraged to create shops using the **Universal Shops** mod.
- Be fair with pricing and avoid scams or misleading trades.
- Use in conjunction with Hey That's Mine.

---

### ⚔️ Factions
- The **Factions** mod is used for grouping and land management.
- Even though factions exist:
  - **PvP is still not allowed**
  - Do not use factions to harass or dominate other players
- Use factions for **organization, cooperation, and shared builds**

---

### 🔒 Container Protection (Hey That's Mine)
- Use **Hey That's Mine** to lock chests, barrels, and containers.
- Respect all locked containers:
  - Do not attempt to bypass protections
  - Do not pressure players for access
- Use in conjunection with Universal Shops.

---

### 🧠 Inventory Sorting
- The server includes **Inventory Sorting** for convenience.
- Use it to keep your inventory organized and efficient.

---

### 🎙️ Voice Chat
- **Proximity Voice Chat** is available.
- Be respectful when speaking—no harassment, spam, or abuse.

---

### 💬 Linked Discord Chat
- Minecraft chat is linked with Discord.
- Follow all Discord rules when communicating in-game.
- Keep chat respectful and appropriate.

---

### ⚙️ Essentials Commands
- Includes commands like `/spawn`, `/home`, `/tpa`, `/rtp`, etc.
- Do not abuse teleportation to gain unfair advantages (e.g., escaping situations improperly).

---

## 🗺️ Web Map Usage
- The live map is available at: https://map.scaredkitty.net
- Do not use the map to grief, track players maliciously, or invade bases.

---

## 📦 Modpack Usage
- Players can use the **official server modpack** for the best possible experience.
- Import it via **CurseForge** for the best experience and compatibility.
- Only QoL modpacks are allowed on the server, please do not use any other mods on the server.

---

## 🚨 Enforcement
- Staff reserves the right to:
  - Remove builds
  - Roll back damage
  - Issue warnings, kicks, or bans

---

## ✅ Final Note
Use common sense.  
If something feels unfair, disruptive, or harmful to the community—it’s probably not allowed.

**Have fun and build something awesome!**
